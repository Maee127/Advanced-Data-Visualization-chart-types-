# Advanced-Data-Visualization-chart-types-
A complete guide to choose a chart to show data;
Show types of chart to show data relationship on Iris & World Population datasets

## 📝 One-Liner Summary for Each Chart:

3D Scatter: "See how three measurements relate in 3D space"

Histogram: "Where do most values fall, and is the data skewed?"

Pie/Donut: "Who has the biggest piece of the pie?"

Bubble: "Three dimensions in one — position + size tell the story"

Sunburst: "How does the hierarchy break down from top to bottom?"

Gauge: "How close are we to the target?"

Pyramid: "How does population split by age and gender?"

Funnel: "Where are we losing people in the process?"

Animated Bar: "Watch the race change over time!"

Area: "How do the stacks grow and shift over time?"
